My Work

Travel Guide

In the aftermath of the COVID-19 pandemic, I identified a common concern that many travelers had: airline reliability.

This case study outlines the UX research steps I took to identify the problem, brainstorm a solution, and then design an app that allows travelers to feel a little more comfortable booking that flight.

Second Chance Chi

While trying to identify a non-profit’s website that needed updating, we found an organization called Second Chance Chi. As a group of people who love dogs and have a passion for rescue animals, we decided that we wanted to redesign their website to increase usability.

This case study outlines the UX research steps we took to identify the problem with the current website, brainstorm a solution, and then design mobile and desktop iterations of their new website.
