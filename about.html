About Me

My friends would definitely describe me as hilarious, witty, and the smartest person they know. While this may be true, I am also hard working, reliable, and a team player.  I have a strong moral drive to understand people. I have worked hard to develop empathy for others. You might even catch me crying on the elliptical at the gym while watching a family episode of Survivor. Now, it is my turn to help find solutions for life’s problems, and make it look good in the process! As a former elementary school teacher, I have developed organization, creativity, and storytelling skills. After deciding to make a career switch, I joined the UI/UX bootcamp through the University Of Utah. I successfully finished the bootcamp in August 2023.
